About

Arezou's academic journey began with a bachelor's degree in computer science, laying the foundation for her focus on bioinformatics. She furthered her studies with a master's program, where she deepened her expertise and practical skills. Her work focuses on identifying biomarkers, predicting gestational age, and studying pregnancy-related diseases.
