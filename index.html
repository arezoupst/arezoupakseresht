About Me

I am Arezou Pakseresht, a passionate explorer at the intersection of computer science and bioinformatics. My aim is to identify biomarkers, predict gestational age, and gain insights into pregnancy-related diseases. My ultimate goal is to contribute positively to healthcare and well-being through innovative research and solutions.
